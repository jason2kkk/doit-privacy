Privacy Policy

Doit - Task Management Application

Last Updated: March 26, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Doit ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any personal information you provide while using our task management application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

2. Information We Don't Collect

We want to be clear that Doit is designed with privacy in mind. We DO NOT collect or process:

  • Calendar information
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Microphone audio
  • Camera images
  • Photo library content
  • Personal identification information
  • Usage statistics
  • Browsing history

3. Information We Do Collect and Store

The only information we store is:

  • Your tasks and their organization within the Eisenhower Matrix
  • Application preferences and settings

All this information is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All task data is stored locally on your device
  • Data is saved in your Documents folder
  • No cloud storage or synchronization is implemented
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • Local data is protected by your device's built-in security measures

5. System Permissions

Our app requires only two system permissions:

  • Accessibility: Required for global hotkey functionality
  • Apple Events: Required for handling keyboard shortcuts

6. Third-Party Services

Doit does not integrate with any third-party services and does not share any information with third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

Our application does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data retention is controlled by you
  • Data can be deleted by removing the application
  • No server-side data exists to be deleted

11. Compliance

This application complies with:

  • Apple App Store Guidelines
  • macOS Privacy Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

12. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by removing the application)
  • Control all aspects of data storage and usage

13. Security Measures

We implement security measures through:

  • Local-only storage
  • System-level file protection
  • No network data transmission
  • No cloud storage

14. Conclusion

Doit is designed to be a privacy-focused task management tool that keeps your data under your control at all times. We believe in transparency and minimal data collection, ensuring that your task management remains private and secure.
