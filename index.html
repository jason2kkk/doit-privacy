Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Do it! – a macOS native task management application designed around the Eisenhower Matrix and conveniently displayed in your menu bar for streamlined time management. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

To safeguard your privacy, Do it! does not collect or process the following types of data:

  • Calendar events
  • Contacts
  • Location data
  • Microphone recordings
  • Camera images
  • Photo library content
  • Personal identification details
  • Usage analytics
  • Browsing history

3. Information We Collect and Store

We only collect information that directly enhances your task management experience:

  • Your tasks and their categorization within the Eisenhower Matrix
  • Application preferences and settings

All data is stored exclusively on your device—in your Documents folder—and is never transmitted to any servers or third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All task-related data remains on your device, ensuring it never leaves your control.
  • File Protection: Our app leverages your system's built-in security measures to safeguard your information.
  • No Network Transmission: No data is transmitted over the internet; there is no cloud synchronization.

5. System Permissions

For optimal functionality, the app requires only two system permissions:

  • Accessibility: Enables global hotkey functionality.
  • Apple Events: Facilitates keyboard shortcut handling.

6. Third-Party Services

Do it! does not integrate with any third-party services and will never share your information with external entities.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not designed for, nor does it collect any information from, individuals under the age of 13, in compliance with applicable data protection regulations.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with an updated "Last Updated" date. Please review this policy periodically to stay informed about any modifications.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Data Ownership: You maintain complete control over all data stored on your device.
  • Deletion Process: Uninstalling the app will remove all local data.
  • No Server Data: Since no data is transmitted or stored on remote servers, there is no need for server-side deletion.

10. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: jason2k@126.com

11. Compliance Statement

Do it! complies with:

  • Apple App Store and macOS privacy and security guidelines
  • Applicable international data protection laws (such as GDPR and CCPA, where applicable)
  • Relevant local regulations and best practices

12. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, manage, and delete any data stored locally on your device
  • Control how your information is collected and used

13. Security Measures

Our security measures include:

  • Local-Only Storage: Your data never leaves your device
  • System-Level Protection: We rely on the robust security features built into your system
  • Zero Network Transmission: No data is shared over the internet

14. Conclusion

At Do it!, we are dedicated to delivering an efficient, privacy-focused task management experience. Your data security and trust remain our top priorities. Thank you for choosing Do it!—we look forward to providing you with a seamless and reliable tool to support your daily productivity.
