Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 22, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Do it! – a task management application based on the Eisenhower Matrix, available for both macOS and iOS platforms. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information while providing an efficient task management experience across all your devices.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

To safeguard your privacy, Do it! does not collect or process the following types of data:

  • Calendar events (except for optional Reminders app integration on iOS)
  • Contacts
  • Location data
  • Microphone recordings
  • Camera images
  • Photo library content
  • Personal identification details
  • Usage analytics
  • Browsing history
  • Device identifiers
  • Network activity

3. Information We Collect and Store

We only collect information that directly enhances your task management experience:

  • Your tasks and their categorization within the Eisenhower Matrix
  • Task priorities, due dates, and completion status
  • Task reminders and repeat settings
  • Application preferences and settings
  • User interface customizations

All data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to any servers or third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All task-related data remains on your device, ensuring it never leaves your control.
  • File Protection: Our app leverages your system's built-in security measures to safeguard your information.
  • No Network Transmission: No data is transmitted over the internet; there is no cloud synchronization.
  • Encryption: Data is protected by iOS/macOS system-level encryption and security features.

5. System Permissions

For optimal functionality, the app may request the following system permissions:

macOS:

  • Accessibility: Enables global hotkey functionality for quick task creation.
  • Apple Events: Facilitates keyboard shortcut handling and automation features.

iOS:

  • Notifications: Sends local notifications for task reminders and due dates.
  • Reminders (Optional): Enables two-way synchronization with the system Reminders app to keep your tasks in sync across Apple devices.
  • Background App Refresh (Optional): Allows the app to update task reminders and sync data when not actively in use.

6. Reminders App Integration (iOS Only)

On iOS, Do it! offers optional integration with Apple's Reminders app:

  • Two-way Sync: When enabled, tasks can be synchronized between Do it! and the Reminders app.
  • User Control: This feature is entirely optional and can be disabled at any time in app settings.
  • Local Processing: All synchronization occurs locally on your device; no data is sent to external servers.
  • Priority Mapping: Task priorities are intelligently mapped between the two apps while preserving your organizational structure.

7. Third-Party Services

Do it! does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. We do not share your information with external entities.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not designed for, nor does it collect any information from, individuals under the age of 13, in compliance with COPPA and applicable data protection regulations.

9. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with an updated "Last Updated" date. Please review this policy periodically to stay informed about any modifications.

10. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Data Ownership: You maintain complete control over all data stored on your device.
  • Deletion Process: Uninstalling the app will remove all local data.
  • No Server Data: Since no data is transmitted or stored on remote servers, there is no need for server-side deletion.
  • Reminders Sync: Disabling Reminders integration will stop synchronization but preserve existing tasks in both apps.

11. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: jason2k@126.com

12. Compliance Statement

Do it! complies with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines for both macOS and iOS
  • Apple's Human Interface Guidelines and privacy requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) where applicable
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Relevant local regulations and best practices

13. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, manage, and delete any data stored locally on your device
  • Control how your information is collected and used
  • Enable or disable optional features like Reminders synchronization
  • Export your task data from the application
  • Request information about data processing practices

14. Security Measures

Our security measures include:

  • Local-Only Storage: Your data never leaves your device
  • System-Level Protection: We rely on the robust security features built into iOS and macOS
  • Zero Network Transmission: No data is shared over the internet
  • Sandboxed Environment: The app operates within Apple's security sandbox
  • Encrypted Storage: Data benefits from iOS/macOS system encryption

15. Platform-Specific Information

macOS Version:

  • Data stored in the application's designated container within your user directory
  • Integrates with macOS menu bar for quick access
  • Utilizes macOS accessibility features for global shortcuts

iOS Version:

  • Data stored in the app's sandbox, protected by iOS security measures
  • Optional integration with iOS Reminders app for cross-device synchronization
  • Local notifications for task reminders and due dates
  • Supports iOS widgets for quick task overview

16. Conclusion

At Do it!, we are dedicated to delivering an efficient, privacy-focused task management experience across all Apple platforms. Your data security and trust remain our top priorities. Thank you for choosing Do it!—we look forward to providing you with a seamless and reliable tool to support your daily productivity while maintaining the highest standards of privacy protection.

隐私政策

最后更新日期：2024年12月22日

1. 引言

欢迎使用 Do it! ——一款基于艾森豪威尔四象限法则的任务管理应用，适用于 macOS 和 iOS 平台。我们致力于保护您的隐私，确保您在使用本应用过程中产生的信息安全，并在所有设备上提供高效的任务管理体验。

2. 我们不收集的信息

为最大程度保障您的隐私，Do it! 不会收集或处理以下数据：

  • 日历数据（除iOS版本可选的提醒事项集成外）
  • 联系人信息
  • 地理位置信息
  • 麦克风录音

  • 使用统计数据
  • 浏览记录

  • 网络活动记录

3. 我们收集和存储的信息

我们仅存储与任务管理及应用个性化相关的信息，包括：

  • 您的任务及其在艾森豪威尔矩阵中的归类
  • 任务优先级、截止日期和完成状态
  • 任务提醒和重复设置
  • 应用偏好设置
  • 用户界面自定义设置

所有数据均仅存储在您的设备本地，绝不会上传至服务器或第三方平台。

4. 数据存储与安全

  • 本地存储： 所有任务数据保存在您设备上，确保信息不出设备。
  • 文件保护： 依赖系统级安全措施保护您的数据。
  • 无网络传输： 数据不会通过互联网传输，也不涉及云端同步。
  • 加密保护： 数据受到 iOS/macOS 系统级加密和安全功能保护。

5. 系统权限

为实现快捷而高效的操作体验，应用可能需要以下权限：

macOS版本：

  • 辅助功能（Accessibility）： 用于全局快捷键功能，快速创建任务。
  • Apple Events： 用于处理键盘快捷操作和自动化功能。

iOS版本：

  • 通知权限： 发送本地通知提醒任务和截止日期。
  • 提醒事项权限（可选）： 启用与系统提醒事项应用的双向同步，在苹果设备间保持任务同步。
  • 后台应用刷新（可选）： 允许应用在后台更新任务提醒和同步数据。

6. 提醒事项集成（仅限iOS）

在 iOS 版本中，Do it! 提供与苹果提醒事项应用的可选集成：

  • 双向同步： 启用后，任务可以在 Do it! 和提醒事项应用之间同步。
  • 用户控制： 此功能完全可选，可随时在应用设置中禁用。
  • 本地处理： 所有同步操作都在您的设备本地进行，不会发送数据至外部服务器。
  • 优先级映射： 任务优先级在两个应用间智能映射，同时保持您的组织结构。

7. 第三方服务

Do it! 不集成任何第三方分析、广告或数据收集服务，也不会将您的数据分享给第三方。

8. 儿童隐私保护

本应用不面向13岁以下用户，也不会收集任何未成年人信息，符合COPPA及相关数据保护法规要求。

  • 数据归属： 您对设备上的数据拥有完全控制权。
